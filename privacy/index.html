Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-22  ·  michalcifra.com

This Privacy Policy describes how Michal Cifra ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in connection with michalcifra.com and associated personal science communication tools.

1. Information I Do Not Collect

michalcifra.com is a static personal website. It does not:

  • Run a server that collects visitor data
  • Use cookies for tracking or advertising
  • Operate a user account or registration system
  • Sell or share any data with third parties

2. Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses Google Fonts. These services may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, page requested) under their own privacy policies. I do not have access to or control over this data.

3. Automated Science Communication Tools

I operate personal automation tools that post scientific video content to social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, and others) on my behalf. These tools:

  • Operate solely under my own platform accounts and authorization
  • Request only the minimum API permissions needed to post content
  • Do not collect, process, or store personal data from other platform users
  • Use OAuth tokens stored securely on private infrastructure; tokens are never shared or published
  • Access platform APIs solely to upload and publish scientific outreach videos to my own accounts

4. OAuth Redirect

The URL michalcifra.com/callback is used as a redirect endpoint during OAuth authorization flows with social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube). No data is stored at this endpoint; it exists solely as a technically required redirect URI for the OAuth protocol.

5. Analytics

This site does not use any analytics tracking scripts (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels).

6. Your Rights

As this site collects no personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of. If you believe any data related to you has been inadvertently collected, contact me and I will address it promptly.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach me at michalcifra.com/#contact or via the Institute of Photonics and Electronics, Czech Academy of Sciences.
